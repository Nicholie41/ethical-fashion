## Key Decision Points:

### Customer Journey:
1. **Registration Decision**: Sign up or continue as guest
2. **Product Selection**: Based on sustainability score, price, brand
3. **Purchase Decision**: Add to cart or continue browsing
4. **Checkout Process**: Complete purchase or abandon cart

### Supplier Journey:
1. **Brand Approval**: Wait for admin approval
2. **Product Approval**: Wait for admin approval
3. **Inventory Management**: Update product availability
4. **Order Fulfillment**: Process customer orders

### Admin Journey:
1. **Approval Decisions**: Approve/reject brands and products
2. **User Management**: Monitor and manage user accounts
3. **Content Moderation**: Review and moderate content
4. **System Maintenance**: Monitor platform health

## Success Metrics:
- **Customer**: Conversion rate, cart abandonment, repeat purchases
- **Supplier**: Approval time, product visibility, sales performance
- **Admin**: Platform health, user satisfaction, system performance
